General interest in government and how patterns and variations in economic inequality and quality of life can be visualised both across countries and across subnational regions and municipalities within countries. Borders are a particularly contentious issue in mainstream media presently; interstate and inter-region boundaries have been very much politicized in places such as the US, Austria, and Ireland. We hope to provide individuals with a more informed position concerning borders in order to enable them to make more conscious political decisions and better understand how the creation or destruction of these man-made divides might impact them
